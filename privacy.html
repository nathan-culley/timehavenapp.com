Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 26, 2026

Timehaven is designed with your privacy in mind. Your data stays on your device. We don't collect, store, or sell your personal information.

What Data Timehaven Uses

Timehaven tracks the following information to provide its core features:

  • Focus session data — session duration, completion status, and timestamps
  • Daily records — total focus minutes and session counts per day
  • Creature and garden state — your companion's mood, health, and garden growth progress
  • App preferences — your creature's name, default session duration, and settings

All of this data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. It never leaves your phone.

What We Don't Collect

Timehaven does not collect, transmit, or store:

  • Your name, email address, or account information (there are no accounts)
  • Your location or contacts
  • Your browsing history or app usage outside of Timehaven
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Analytics or usage telemetry

Screen Time Data

Timehaven uses Apple's Screen Time APIs (DeviceActivity framework) to understand your phone usage patterns. This data is processed entirely on your device by Apple's framework and is never sent to our servers or any third party. We do not have servers.

Subscriptions

If you subscribe to Timehaven Premium, the transaction is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We receive confirmation that you have an active subscription, but we do not receive your payment details, Apple ID, or billing information. See Apple's Privacy Policy for details on how Apple handles purchases.

Third-Party Services

Timehaven does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. The only third-party service involved is Apple's StoreKit for subscription management.

Children's Privacy

Timehaven is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. Since no personal data is collected from any user, there is no children's data at risk.

Data Retention

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it. Deleting the app removes all Timehaven data from your device. There is nothing stored on external servers to delete.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the revised version on this page with a new effective date. Significant changes will be communicated through the app.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us at support@timehavenapp.com.
